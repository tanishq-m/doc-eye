# Media Buying Standards

## Budget Allocation
- Social Media: 40%
- Search Ads: 35%
- Display: 15%
- Other: 10%

## Platform Guidelines

### Facebook/Instagram
- Target CPC: $1.50
- Target ROAS: 3:1
- Daily budget floor: $500

### Google Ads
- Target CPC: $2.00
- Target conversion rate: 2.5%
- Quality score minimum: 7

### LinkedIn
- Target CPC: $3.00
- Focus on B2B campaigns
- Content engagement threshold: 2%

## Performance Thresholds
- Minimum CTR: 0.5%
- Maximum CPA: $25
- Minimum ROAS: 2:1
